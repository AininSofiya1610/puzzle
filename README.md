## 🎮 About

An interactive drag-and-drop ocean puzzle game designed for kids aged 9–12, built for **Kedah Medical Center** as part of a kids' educational games collection.

Kids drag and drop puzzle pieces to complete beautiful ocean creature illustrations — complete with background music, sound effects, and difficulty levels they can choose themselves!

## 🎯 How to Play

1. **Choose difficulty** — pick 4, 9, 16 or 25 pieces

2. **Choose a puzzle** — Clownfish, Turtle, Jellyfish, Octopus or Dolphin

3. **Drag pieces** from the left panel onto the puzzle board

4. **Drop on the right spot** — green outline means correct ✅

5. **Wrong spot?** No problem — drag it back and try again!

6. **Use hints** if stuck (costs +3 moves)

7. **Complete the puzzle** to hear the win fanfare 🎉
